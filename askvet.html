Question: Our dog, Sparky, likes to eat whatever the kids are snacking on. Is it OK for the dog to eat chocolate?
Answer: Chocolate is toxic to dogs. Please do not feed your dog choclate. Try playing a game with the your children — when you feed them people treats, they can feed Sparky dog treats.
